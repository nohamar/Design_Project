About Us ★

Dee runs a cozy thrift shop filled with vintage clothes, décor, and timeless pieces, all carefully curated to give old treasures new life. She knows her customers personally, sharing stories behind special finds and helping them create unique looks. More than a store, her shop is a warm community space built on sustainability, creativity, and connection..
