About Us

Dee runs a cozy thrift shop filled with carefully curated vintage clothes, décor, and timeless finds—all chosen to give old treasures a meaningful new life. What began as her love for thrifting has grown into a welcoming community space where customers come not just to shop, but to connect, share stories, and discover pieces that reflect their unique style. Dee knows her customers personally and loves helping them create looks that celebrate individuality and conscious living.

While finishing her final year as a marketing graduate, Dee blends creativity and strategy to shape the brand with intention, from storytelling to online presence. She runs the shop alongside her significant other, who manages logistics and operations behind the scenes. Together, they balance their strengths to keep the shop running smoothly and to bring their shared vision to life.

Rooted in sustainability, creativity, and genuine connection, their shop invites everyone to explore, feel inspired, and find something special with a story worth continuing.
